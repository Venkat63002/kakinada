Bikkavolu Ganapati Mandir, Andhra Pradesh

Bikkavolu, located in East Godavari district, Andhra, is home to a number of beautiful temples built during the reign of the Chalukyas. The Vinayaka Temple here is a very famous temple built during the 9th century. The monolithic idol of Lord Ganesha here stands at about 7 feet. Legend has it that a farmer discovered it as a Swayambhu (self-originated idol) while ploughing a field. Hence a temple was constructed in the same place to enshrine the deity. Coringa Wildlife Sanctuary is a mangrove ecosystem located in Andhra Pradesh, India, near the Godavari River delta. It's known for its rich biodiversity, including various mangrove species, diverse birdlife, and endangered animals like the Smooth-coated Otter and Fishing Cat. The sanctuary is also a breeding ground for the Olive Ridley sea turtle.
