Putharekulu

Atreyapuram is a village in Konaseema district of Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is located in Atreyapuram Mandal of Amalapuram revenue division of the district. Atreyapuram Putharekulu is a traditional Andhra Pradesh sweet, specifically from the village of Atreyapuram, known for its unique, paper-thin, and crispy layers. It's essentially a rice flour sheet filled with a mixture of jaggery (or sugar) and dry fruits like cashews, almonds, and pistachios, often coated with ghee. The name "Putharekulu" translates to "coated sheets". This delicacy is popular for special occasions and festivals due to its delicious taste and attractive appearance. This sweet is made from a thin rice starch sheet filled with a mixture of jaggery, ghee, and dry fruits. The delicate and intricate process of making Pootharekulu has been a specialty of Atreyapuram for generations.
